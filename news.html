Tested by the EPA for Homeland Security, Sanuvox's Bio-Wall Max is proven to eliminate pathogens with harmless UV air purification. HEPA Filter

High Efficiency Particulate Absorber (HEPA) Filters are specifically designed to filter pollutants and contaminants that pass through them. This is particularly relevant for any particles that may contain viruses. The EPA has stated that these filters can "reduce airborne contaminant including viruses in a building, [and] when used along with other best practices recommended by CDC and others, filtration can be part of a plan to protect people indoors" [Source]. This has been verified, as the RNA from other viruses that are transmitted through the same methods have previously been found on filters.
